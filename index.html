Our Mission

A student’s educational journey is unique for each individual and so are our services. At JWCC, we offer bespoke educational planning that considers the holistic needs of our clients as we guide them through the college application process and beyond.

Global Network

With JWCC representatives in Asia, North America, and the United Kingdom - our close network enables us to provide clients with support across multiple timezones, providing a service that is accessible at your convenience. The resources of our network are at your disposal and students benefit from our collective expertise.

Individual Attention

We take a personal approach to consultancy and believe that our strongest asset is the personal rapport that we establish with our students. At JWCC you are never a statistic and our personalized academic plans are created with the individual in mind. We limit the number of students each counselor works with to ensure that we really connect with our students.

Personal Development

Our advisory approach means that we act as mentors to nurture a proactive ethos and ensure that students are equipped to pursue lifelong success. The JWCC methodology focuses on holistic student development, helping identify academic and personal goals. JWCC places great importance on aftercare and we continue to support our clients post-acceptance and on into college.

Our Services

First

ACADEMIC STRATEGY AND PLANNING

We help our students maximize their potentials by implementing detailed academic strategies based on their unique profiles and goals. We offer clear advice on academics, summer programs, and extracurriculars.

Second

COLLEGE LIST - RESEARCH AND PREPARATION

Based on their profile we help our students carry out intensive college research to ensure that the colleges they choose are right for them. Using our layering system, we prepare a structured college list that is maximized for success.

Third

COLLEGE APPLICATIONS - INCLUDING PERSONAL STATEMENTS AND SUPPLEMENTAL ESSAYS

We help our students complete their applications step-by-step, support is key. Our comprehensive college essay process enables us to fully support our students from brainstorming through to completion whilst maintaining professional integrity.

Fourth

ACCEPTANCE SUPPORT - INCLUDING WAITLISTS

Our relationship doesn't end with application submission. We continue to help our students process their college decisions to ensure that they make the right decision for them.
